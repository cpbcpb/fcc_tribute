Rachel Carson

"Those who dwell among the beauties and mysteries of the earth
are never alone or weary of life."

Rachel Carson outside with binoculars, in 1961.

Rachel Carson was an American marine biologist and writer. She wrote Silent Spring, the influential book that started an environmental movement and saved the bald eagle.

Timeline of Publication of the Writings of Rachel Carson

  • 1941 Under the Sea-Wind
  • 1951 The Sea Around Us
  • 1955 The Edge of the Sea
  • 1962 Silent Spring
  • 1965 The Sense of Wonder
  • 1998 Lost Woods: The Discovered Writings of Rachel Carson
